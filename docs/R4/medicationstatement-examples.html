11.4.5 Resource MedicationStatement - Examples

Pharmacy Work GroupMaturity Level: N/AStandards Status: InformativeSecurity Category: Patient Compartments: Patient, Practitioner, RelatedPerson

The following are examples of Medication Statement resources:

  • Patient reports they are taking Tylenol PM and has the bottle
  • Patient reports they are not taking Tylenol PM
  • Patient reports they took Amoxycillin in the past but are not taking it now
  • Patient reports they previous said they took Amoxycillin but really didn’t
  • Care Giver reports that Patient took Amoxycillin
  • Patient reports that they intend to use Mometasone in the future
  • Patient reports they are taking the “little pink pill for water retention
