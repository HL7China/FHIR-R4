11.13 Resource MedicinalProductContraindication - Content

工作组: Biomedical Research and Regulation 成熟度: 0 Trial UseSecurity Category: Anonymous Compartments: Not linked to any defined compartments

The clinical particulars - indications, contraindications etc. of a medicinal product, including for regulatory purposes.

11.13.1 Scope and Usage

A reason why a medicinal product is not to be used in a certain context
