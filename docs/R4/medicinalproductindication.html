11.14 Resource MedicinalProductIndication - Content

工作组:Biomedical Research and Regulation 成熟度: 0 Trial UseSecurity Category: Anonymous Compartments: Not linked to any defined compartments

Indication for the Medicinal Product.

11.14.1 Scope and Usage

A medical situation in which a medicinal product is appropriate for use

This resource is referenced by MedicinalProduct and MedicinalProductContraindication
